Community Hub

The community parent hub offers a gathering place where parents or caregivers can connect, share information, and receive support from other parents. This hub can offer a variety of services, such as parenting classes, workshops, referrals, playgroups, meet ups, exchange of ideas and access to community resources. The goal of the community parent hub is to create a supportive network of parents and caregivers to promote the well-being and healthy development of children in the home-schooling community.

Social Media

The social media hub aggregates content from various social media platforms, such as Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and WhatsApp, into a single feed or display. It allows users to curate and display user-generated content from multiple sources in one central location. It will be used by the users to showcase user-generated content, engaging with their audience..
